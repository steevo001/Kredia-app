# Kredia Design System

## Typography
- Primary Font (Headings): Fraunces, serif (used for h1, h2, h3). Emphasized words are often italicized.
- Secondary Font (Body): DM Sans, sans-serif

## Color Palette
- Background: #f5f2ec
- Secondary Background: #edeae2
- Surface / Cards: #ffffff
- Primary Text: #1a1812
- Muted Text: #7a7568
- Faint Text: #b0a99e
- Borders: rgba(0,0,0,0.08) and rgba(0,0,0,0.12)

### Accent Colors (Modules)
- Barter Accent: #c8860a
- Barter Light: #fef3d8
- Brain Accent: #1a6e4a
- Brain Light: #d4eee1
- Grid Accent: #2a4fc8
- Grid Light: #d3daf8

## Shapes & Shadows
- Border Radius: Buttons and cards should use very rounded corners (e.g., 999px for buttons, 20px-24px for large cards, 10px-14px for smaller elements).
- Shadows: Soft, diffused shadows on cards and buttons.

## UI Style
- Premium, editorial feel. 
- High contrast typography with bold serifs for headings and clean sans-serif for body.
- Use glassmorphism or slight transparency (e.g. `rgba(245,242,236,0.88)`) for sticky navbars.
